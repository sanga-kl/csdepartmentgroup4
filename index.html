Welcome to the computer science department
Established in January 2015, our Computer Science Department focuses on producing globally competitive professionals for the booming IT industry. As the faculty's youngest department, we offer courses in scientific and theoretical computing, with a special focus on emerging trends in Artificial Intelligence. Our pride lies in offering two major programs: BSc (Hons) in Computer Science and BSc (Hons) in Software Engineering, designed for GCE A/L physical science students. We actively engage in extensive research in Theoretical Computing and AI, aiming to foster a robust research culture across the faculty.
